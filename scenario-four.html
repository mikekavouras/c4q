Baseball Is Life

You're a die hard Mets fan and everyone knows. You can't remember the last time your socks weren't blue and orange. You've had season passes for the past 3 years, and before that you would sneak into games with your brother, Joe. Due to recent life events, you now have less time to devote to The Mets, however, your love remains strong.

Being the great programmer that you are, you decide make your world a little bit easier. You head to Home Depot to buy some Philips Hue light bulbs.

Whenever The Mets are about to play, you want to make sure that the mood is set. You write a program that changes all the lights in your house to glow orange and blue. Also, you want to receive a text message at the same time, letting you know that the show is about to start.

Which APIs could you use to build your program?
