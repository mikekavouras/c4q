Baseball Is Life

You're a die hard Mets fan and everyone knows. You can't remember the last time your socks weren't blue and orange. You've had a season pass for the last three years, and before that you would sneak into games with your brother Joe. Unfortunately, a new job leaves you with less free time.

Using your skills as a programmer you decide to make your surrounding a little bit smarter. You head to Home Depot to buy some Philips Hue light bulbs.

Whenever The Mets are about to play you want to make sure that the mood is set. You write a program that changes all the lights in your house to glow orange and blue. Also, you want to receive a text message letting you know that the game is about to start.

Which APIs could you use to build your program?
